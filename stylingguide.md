# Style Guide

## Colors

### Primary

Night indigo: hsl(257, 17%, 13%)
Muted medium brown: hsla(14, 27%, 50%, 0.66)
Orange-brown: hsl(14, 56%, 41%)
Lighter cold orange: darksalmon

### Neutral

Transparent white (paragraphs): hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.75)
--- (background, headings, buttons): hsl(-, -, -)
